About Me

Explore the World of Donna Mae

Growing up in a family of teachers, I've always been drawn to teaching and sharing what I've learned with others. I've become a vessel for new information and experiences. From a very young age, I felt an intense urge to atomize what I've ingested through writing. Over the years, my exposure to different writing styles, such as songwriting, poetry, blogging, research papers, technical writing, marketing, and so many more, wakened my curiosity in finding the juxtaposition and seeking my purpose.

I never saw writing as only a form of expression but a way to inspire, educate, resonate, and even heal. Every word was chosen with the intention of resonating with others, offering a temporary patch to someone's healing wound.

I write because of the creative freedom I get to express openly, the opportunity to connect with others, and the joy of discovering that I can do all things life brings me. This is what makes my soul dance. I encourage everyone to allow their creativity to flirt with their minds.
